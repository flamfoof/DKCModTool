Dokapon Kingdom Damage Guide

General Rules: Damage is multiplied by a Random factor (0.95 or 1.05). Proficiency adds a 1.3x multiplier to Attack, Strike, and Counter. Final values are rounded to the nearest whole number.

1. Battle Damage Formulas

Standard Attack

Every 2.33 points of Defender DF negates 1 point of Attacker AT.

Damage = (AttackerAT * 2.8 - DefenderDF * 1.2) * Guard * Proficiency * Random

Strike

Sum of stats (AT + MG + SP) vs (DF + MG + SP).

Damage = (AttackerTotal * 2.5 - DefenderTotal) * Guard * Proficiency * Random

Counter (vs Strike)

Triggered when a defender selects "Counter" against a "Strike".

Damage = (DefenderTotal * 4.0 - AttackerTotal) * Proficiency * Random

2. Field & Overworld

  • Damage Spells: Scales primarily via MG vs MG.
  • Status Effects: Poison deals flat or % based HP reduction.
  • Accuracy: Hit% = (AttackerSP - DefenderSP) + Base Spell Accuracy.
