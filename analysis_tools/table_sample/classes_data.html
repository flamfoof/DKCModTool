General Job Mechanics

  • Salary: Your weekly pay increases by a multiplier every time your job levels up.
  • Item Slots: Different jobs can carry different amounts of Items and Field Magic (usually 5 to 10 slots).
  • Weapon Proficiency: Jobs gain a 1.3x damage bonus when using their preferred weapon types (e.g., Warriors with Swords).
